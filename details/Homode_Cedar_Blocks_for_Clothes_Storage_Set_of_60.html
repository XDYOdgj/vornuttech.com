Homode Cedar Blocks for Natural Clothes Storage

Keep your wardrobe looking fresh and pest-free with these premium cedar blocks made from 100% U.S. eastern red cedar. Ideal for both residential and commercial use, they provide a safe, chemical-free alternative to keep your garments, linens, and fabrics protected from moths and musty odors.

Why Choose Homode Cedar Blocks?

Our cedar blocks are naturally effective at repelling moths and other insects, ensuring your clothes remain in excellent condition for longer. The pleasant forest scent not only protects but also adds a subtle, organic aroma to your storage areas.

Each set includes 60 pieces, giving you the flexibility to store your items in different places such as closets, drawers, and storage bags. The smooth surface of the cedar blocks means they won't damage your fabrics or cause scratches on delicate garments like wool or silk.

What Makes Homode Cedar Blocks Stand Out?

In addition to being an excellent natural moth repellent, these cedar storage products are designed for ease of use. No assembly required, they come ready to place wherever you need them. The inclusion of sandpaper allows you to easily restore the cedar scent when needed, enhancing the freshness of your stored items.

Whether you're organizing your closet, protecting your winter clothes, or storing seasonal items, these cedar blocks offer a simple, effective, and long-lasting solution. They're a perfect addition to your wardrobe care routine without the need for synthetic chemicals.
