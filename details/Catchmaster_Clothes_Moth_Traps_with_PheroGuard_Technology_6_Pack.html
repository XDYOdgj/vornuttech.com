Keep Your Wardrobe Safe from Moths with Catchmaster Traps

Catchmaster clothes moth traps are designed with advanced PheroGuard Technology to attract and eliminate moths efficiently. These traps target both Webbing and Case Making moths, which are the most common culprits for damaging your clothes and fabrics. Free from harsh chemicals, they are a safe and effective solution for homes, ensuring your family and pets are never exposed to harmful substances.

With a durable build and powerful extra-strength glue, these moth traps offer long-lasting and reliable performance. Ideal for use in closets, wardrobes, drawers, and even attics, they help prevent infestations and protect your valuable items from the destruction caused by moths. The unique grid design makes it easy to monitor trapped moths and keep track of infestation levels, giving you peace of mind and control.
